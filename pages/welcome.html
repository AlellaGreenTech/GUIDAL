Welcome to Alella Green Tech!

We are thrilled to host you at our living laboratory at Can Picard Farm, where young minds like yours can see science in action and explore the exciting world of green technology and permaculture. As a branch of the International Green Tech Foundation, our mission is to inspire the next generation of green-minded scientists and engineers through hands-on learning experiences and real-world applications.

This app serves as your comprehensive guide to our sustainable technology demonstrations, innovative projects, and personalized learning stations, designed to enhance your understanding of environmental science, ecology, and sustainable practices.

Get ready for a day of discovery, from robotics farming and smart irrigation to wattle and daub construction and the erosion challenge, where you'll engage directly with nature and technology to build a greener future!
