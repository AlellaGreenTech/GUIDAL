Halloween Party 2025 🎃👻

📅 Date: Saturday, October 25, 2025

🕐 Time: 12:30 PM until late

📍 Location: Can Picard Educational Farm, Camí Baix de Tiana 2B, Alella

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Perfect for: Families, Kids & Halloween Enthusiasts

Experience Halloween at Can Picard!

Join us for a family-friendly Halloween celebration featuring pumpkin picking, carving, wood-fired pizza, wine tasting, and festive fun at our educational farm. Choose between two amazing dates - October 25th or November 1st!

🎃 What Makes This Special?

  • Educational farm setting with sustainable focus
  • Interactive Halloween experience for all ages
  • Combines fun with environmental awareness
  • Support Alella Green Tech Foundation's mission

Halloween Activities

🎃 Pumpkin Patch

Pick your perfect pumpkin from our farm-grown patch. Each family can select pumpkins to take home or carve on-site.

🔪 Pumpkin Carving

Join our supervised pumpkin carving station. Tools and guidance provided for creating your spooky masterpiece!

🍕 Wood Fire Pizza

Enjoy authentic wood-fired pizza made with locally-sourced ingredients throughout the afternoon.

🍷 Wine Tasting

Adults can sample our local wines while the kids enjoy activities and games.

🎉 Halloween Party

Festive music, games, and Halloween-themed activities throughout the day until late evening.

📸 Photo Gallery

Capture memories at our Halloween photo stations. Check out photos from previous years!

Get Your Tickets

Secure your spot for this popular family event. Tickets include entrance and access to all activities. Pumpkin coupons available separately.

💡 Pro Tip

About Alella Green Tech Foundation

We promote sustainable living education through our Can Picard Educational Farm, focusing on youth engagement and climate change innovation. Our events combine fun, learning, and environmental stewardship.
